what is differece between Trapping rainwater and Container with Most Water 

1) Trapping rainwater 

The trapping rain water problem calculates the total volume of water stored between bars of varying heights, where each bar has a width of 1, after it rains.

2) Container with Most Water

The "Container with Most Water" problem asks to find two vertical lines in an array (representing heights) that, together with the x-axis, form a container holding the maximum water.

Apporach of both problems are differents
